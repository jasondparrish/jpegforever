About Us

The story behind JPEG Forever LLC

Who We Are

JPEG Forever LLC is an independent game and app development studio founded in 2026 and based in Saint Johns, Florida. We're a small team of developers passionate about creating software that people genuinely enjoy using.

Our work spans mobile games, desktop games, and utility applications. We believe that whether you're playing a game to relax or using an app to get something done, the experience should be smooth, polished, and thoughtfully designed.

Our Approach

We take a quality-first approach to development. Rather than rushing to release as many titles as possible, we focus on making each project the best it can be. This means thorough testing, attention to user feedback, and regular updates to improve our apps after launch.

We're also committed to respecting our users. That means no shady data practices, no manipulative design patterns, and clear communication about what our apps do and how they work.

Our Values

Quality

We ship when it's ready, not when it's rushed. Polish matters.

Respect

We respect our users' time, attention, and privacy.

Fun

Games should be fun. Apps should be useful. That's the whole point.

Transparency

We're clear about what our software does and how it works.
