Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

JPEG Forever LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates mobile applications and games. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our apps.

App-Specific Disclosures

Roid-Combat

Roid-Combat is an offline arcade game. It stores game settings and high scores locally on your device only. No personal data is collected or transmitted. Roid-Combat displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob; see "Third-Party Services" below for more information.

PicOut

PicOut is a picture puzzle game that accesses photos stored on your device. All photos remain on your device and are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared. PicOut does not collect personal data. PicOut displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob; see "Third-Party Services" below for more information.

Invasion 3000

Invasion 3000 is a free offline space shooter game. It does not collect any personal data, does not contain advertisements, and does not require an internet connection.

Information We Collect

The information we collect depends on the specific app you use:

  • Apps without accounts: Some of our apps do not require an account and collect no personal information.
  • Apps with accounts: If you create an account, we may collect your name and email address to provide and manage your account.
  • Device information: Some apps may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app performance data to help us improve our apps.
  • Advertising: Some of our free apps display ads provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob. These partners may collect device identifiers and usage data to show relevant ads. See "Third-Party Services" below for more information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our apps
  • Manage your account if you create one
  • Improve and optimize our apps
  • Communicate with you about updates or support
  • Display advertisements in free versions of our apps

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: With third-party services that help us operate our apps (such as advertising partners), subject to their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Third-Party Services

Some of our apps use third-party services that may collect information. These include:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
Note: Third-party advertising applies only to JPEG Forever LLC applications that display ads. Not all applications include advertisements.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • You can delete your account (if applicable) by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.
  • You can uninstall our apps at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.
